FFXIV REPOSITORY ACCURACY AUDIT
Audit date: 2026-06-18
Game baseline: Patch 7.5

Scope
-----
Reviewed all six Markdown files in this repository. Existing files were not
modified. Detailed findings are in the corresponding *_accuracy_issues.txt
files.

Overall result
--------------

UNSAFE / EXTENSIVE ERRORS

1. ffxiv_tank_duty_cheatsheet.md
2. ffxiv_healer_duty_cheatsheet.md
3. ffxiv_dps_duty_cheatsheet.md

These three guides repeatedly assign bosses to the wrong dungeon, put bosses
in the wrong order, describe mechanics belonging to other encounters, invent
mechanics, and omit actual bosses. The errors are systematic from ARR through
Endwalker, not isolated typos. The Dawntrail named-boss entries also contain
errors. These files should not be used as duty guides without a rewrite based
on encounter-specific sources.

MOSTLY ACCURATE / LIMITED OR NO CONFIRMED ERRORS

4. ffxiv_poetics_and_artifact_gear_ladder.md
   One misleading recommendation was found; the factual gear ladder checked
   out through Patch 7.5.

5. ffxiv_squadron_notes.md
   The progression gates and class-change facts checked out. Some tactical
   recommendations are opinion rather than verifiable fact; one Ungarmax claim
   needs qualification.

6. ffxiv_sirclucks_market_board_farming_notes.md
   The named drops, zones, recipes, and retainer venture claims checked out.
   No confirmed factual error was found in the audited claims.

Important repeated errors
-------------------------

- Physical-ranged jobs (BRD, MCH, DNC) cannot raise party members.
- Weakness does not increase damage taken; it reduces main attributes.
- DPS jobs no longer have general enmity-drop actions.
- Plenary Indulgence must be used before the qualifying AoE heal, not after.
- Liturgy of the Bell triggers from damage taken by the White Mage, not from
  the bell itself being attacked.
- Numerous ARR mechanics use pre-rework or completely unrelated information.
- Many dungeon boss rosters from Heavensward through Endwalker are incorrect.
