# FFXIV Miner Fieldcraft Leve Locations (1–100)

Purpose: quick routing sheet for leveling Miner and burning leve allowances.

Important:
- These are **leve pickup NPC coordinates**.
- For Heavensward onward, the table also includes the **objective location/zone** when listed.
- The actual mining nodes are not usually worth pre-coord hunting. Accept the leve, hit **Begin**, then follow the marked area and use **Prospect / Lay of the Land**.
- There is no separate level 100 leve tier. **Level 98 leves carry you to 100.**
- ARR/local levemetes may require the local **“Leves of [camp]”** unlock first, usually on a combat job.

## Notes

### Do not forget to start the leve

Accepting it is not enough.

Open the leve from your journal and hit **Begin**. Otherwise the leve nodes/objective won't spawn properly, and you get to wander around like the game stole your glasses.

### Node-finding checklist

1. Be on Miner.
2. Equip pickaxe and sledgehammer.
3. Turn on **Prospect**.
4. Hit **Begin** on the leve.
5. Use **Lay of the Land**.
6. Watch the minimap and the leve objective area.

### Practical leveling priority

For low-level Miner leve burning:

1. Keep tools current.
2. Prioritize Gathering.
3. Use cheap vendor/quest gear.
4. Do not dump serious gil into low-level gear.
5. Move to the next leve tier as soon as the XP starts looking weak or you qualify for the next hub.

### Map money breakpoint

Your first big utility breakpoint is **Miner 40**, because that opens the path toward gathering and selling Timeworn Leather Maps, assuming your market is still paying well for them.
