# FFXIV MSQ Gear Ladder: Poetics + Free Artifact Gear

Practical gearing note for running MSQ without thinking too hard every few levels.

Scope: combat jobs, especially **Warrior / tank MSQ progression**. This is not savage gearing, BiS planning, materia optimization, or spreadsheet monk behavior. It is the lazy-correct leveling ladder.

## Core idea

Every old expansion cap has two useful gear concepts:

1. **Artifact/job/role gear**
   - Free job-looking armor from quests or vendors.
   - Good bridge gear.
   - Often very good glamour.
   - Usually not the final best old-expansion set.

2. **Augmented Poetics gear**
   - Bought with **Allagan Tomestones of Poetics**.
   - Usually the best lazy handoff set at old expansion caps.
   - Carries you comfortably into the next expansion.

The loop:

```text
Hit x0 / reach relevant MSQ unlock
→ grab free artifact/job/role armor if available
→ buy augmented Poetics weapon + armor when vendor unlocks
→ ignore gear for a while
→ replace only obvious upgrades
→ repeat at next x0
```

Stay near the **2,000 Poetics cap** before the next vendor unlock so you can buy the important pieces immediately.

## Buy priority for Poetics gear

For one main job:

1. **Weapon**
2. **Chest + legs**
3. **Head + hands + feet**
4. **Accessories**
5. **Second ring cleanup / leftovers**

Why:

- Weapon damage matters a lot.
- Chest and legs are huge stat chunks.
- Accessories are useful, but not worth delaying weapon/core armor.
- Tome rings are often `Unique`, so you may not be able to wear two identical augmented rings. Use the best second ring you naturally get from MSQ/dungeons/vendors. Do not lose sleep over this.

For Warrior, buy the **Fending** set.

## Important job-quest change after 70

Level 70 is basically the end of the old-style job quest era.

For Warrior:

```text
60 → 63 → 65 → 68 → 70
```

The level 70 WAR quest is **The Heart of the Problem**.

It gives:

- **Brutal Armor Coffer (IL 290)**
- **Inner Release**
- **Berserk Mastery**

After that, Square mostly moves from job-specific quest chains to **role quests**.

So the pattern becomes:

```text
70 and earlier:
WAR job quests → WAR armor/skills

80+:
Role quests / MSQ unlocks → free artifact armor vendors
```

Blunt version:

```text
70 is the last "Curious Gorge hands me WAR homework and armor" tier.
80+ is "finish role/MSQ requirements, then pick up free job armor from vendor."
```

## Level 70 practical note

At 70, the **Brutal Armor Coffer** is good bridge gear if you hit 70 before you can buy **Augmented Scaevan**.

But:

```text
Brutal Armor Coffer: ilvl 290
Augmented Scaevan:   ilvl 400
```

So once Scaevan is available, Scaevan deletes it for actual stats.

Keep the Brutal set for glamour/collection if you like the look.

## Level 80 practical note

At 80, Shadowbringers artifact gear is free and good enough as a bridge, but **Augmented Cryptlurker** is the actual lazy power set.

```text
Shadowbringers artifact: ilvl 430
Augmented Cryptlurker:   ilvl 530
```

Use the free job armor when it unlocks. Replace with Cryptlurker once available.

## Level 90 practical note

Endwalker artifact armor unlocks around late Endwalker MSQ from **Varsarudh in Old Sharlayan** after **A Bold Decision**.

It syncs:

```text
Level 89: ilvl 548
Level 90: ilvl 560
```

It is free and intended to get you through the late MSQ wall without your pants being made of cardboard.

Replace with **Augmented Credendum** when available:

```text
Endwalker artifact at 90: ilvl 560
Augmented Credendum:     ilvl 660
```

Role quests unlock dyeing for this armor.

## Level 100 practical note

Dawntrail artifact armor unlocks from **Mewazunte in Tuliyollal** after **In Pursuit of Sphene**.

It syncs:

```text
Level 99:  ilvl 678
Level 100: ilvl 690
```

This is current-expansion territory, so there is no lazy Poetics replacement yet.

At 100, gear through whatever current Dawntrail systems are active:

- MSQ/job artifact gear
- dungeons
- crafted gear
- current tomestones
- raids/alliance raids
- whatever current patch catch-up system exists

Role quests unlock dyeing for this armor.

## Replacement rule while leveling

Do not farm replacement gear while MSQ leveling unless the game blocks you with an item-level requirement.

Use this rule:

```text
If a dungeon/MSQ reward is higher item level, equip it.
If it is not higher item level, ignore it unless the stats are wildly better.
If it is a weapon upgrade, care more.
```

Poetics gear is strongest right at the old expansion cap. Dungeon/MSQ gear usually starts catching up somewhere around the middle/back half of the next expansion.

That is fine. The point is not to be perfectly geared. The point is to avoid gear panic.

## Poetics cap strategy

Since Poetics cap at 2,000:

- Sit around **1,500–2,000** before the next vendor unlock.
- When the set unlocks, buy weapon/chest/legs first.
- Run roulettes/MSQ/dailies to fill out the rest.
- If capped early, buy:
  - alt-job gear for likely future jobs,
  - glamour pieces,
  - GC seal conversion items,
  - relic materials only if you know you need them.

Do not let perfect efficiency turn into teleport-fee clown math.

## Warrior-specific lazy rules

For WAR during MSQ:

- Buy **Fending** gear.
- Prioritize the axe.
- Higher item level beats substat nitpicking while leveling.
- Do not worry about Tenacity vs Skill Speed unless you are doing current endgame optimization.
- If survivability feels bad, check weapon/chest/legs first.
- If threat feels weird, it is probably not the gear unless your weapon is ancient.

## Practical Rhela checklist

At each cap:

```text
[ ] Do available WAR/job/role quest stuff
[ ] Claim free artifact/job/role gear if unlocked
[ ] Unlock relevant tomestone vendor
[ ] Buy WAR/Fending weapon
[ ] Buy Fending chest
[ ] Buy Fending legs
[ ] Buy remaining left-side armor
[ ] Buy accessories
[ ] Fill second ring slot with best available
[ ] Keep artifact gear for glam if it looks cool
[ ] Stop caring until the next x0 cap
```
