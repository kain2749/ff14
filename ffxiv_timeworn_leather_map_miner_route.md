# FFXIV Timeworn Leather Map Miner Route

Purpose: fast daily route for gathering one Timeworn Leather Map with Miner once Miner reaches level 40.

## Core Rule

Timeworn Leather Maps come from **level 40 gathering nodes**.

For Miner, use level 40 **Mineral Deposit** or **Rocky Outcrop** nodes. Do not farm level 45/50 nodes if you specifically want Leather Maps, because those nodes can point you toward different map tiers.

Maps are limited by the normal treasure map gathering timer: once you gather a timeworn map, you cannot gather another one for about **18 hours**. You can still buy maps from the Market Board.

## Best Miner Coordinates

| Priority | Zone | Area | Node type | Coordinates | Notes |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Coerthas Central Highlands | Dragonhead | Lv. 40 Mineral Deposit | X:24.2 Y:19.9 | Best first route. Teleport to Camp Dragonhead and rotate nearby deposits. |
| 2 | South Shroud | Urth's Gift | Lv. 40 Mineral Deposit | X:28.6 Y:22.7 | Good backup. Teleport to Quarrymill, then head east/northeast. |
| 3 | Northern Thanalan | Bluefog | Lv. 40 Rocky Outcrop | X:21.7 Y:27.9 | Valid, but less pleasant terrain. Requires sledgehammer/off-hand. |

## Recommended Daily Route

1. Teleport to **Camp Dragonhead**.
2. Go to roughly **X:24.2 Y:19.9**.
3. Turn on Miner and make sure **Prospect / Auto Prospect** is active.
4. Rotate the nearby **level 40 Mineral Deposits**.
5. Open each node and check whether **Timeworn Leather Map** appears.
6. If the map is not there, close the node and move to the next one.
7. When the map appears, gather it.
8. Sell it, decipher/use it, or stash it.
9. Wait for the treasure map timer before gathering another.

## Practical Notes

- The map is the target, not the ore. Do not waste time gathering random materials unless you want them.
- If a node does not show the map, move on immediately.
- Later, Miner gets tools for revealing hidden items, but at level 40 the main method is just cycling eligible nodes quickly.
- If Leather Maps are selling well on your server, this is a simple daily gil faucet.
- If the Dragonhead route feels annoying, use Urth's Gift as the backup.
- Bluefog works, but it is the least comfortable of the three routes.
