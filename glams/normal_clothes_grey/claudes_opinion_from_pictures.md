# FFXIV Glam — Character Read

**Outfit:** Grey crew-neck tee, dark wash flare jeans, beanie pulled low, ombré pink-to-white hair in a thick single braid/dread, minimal jewelry (chunky necklace, bracelet, rings). Functional dark loafers, not boots. Hauling a battle-axe roughly her own body length.

**Read:**

Deliberate mismatch between outfit and weapon. The fit is streetwear — could pass for someone's barista or skate shop employee — set against a job that involves splitting people in half with a war axe. That's not a coordination failure, it's a statement: doesn't dress for the part, doesn't perform "hero." No armor, no faction colors, no ostentation. Beanie and loose silhouette read as "doesn't want to be looked at," which is funny set against canonically being the one person every NPC in the room is staring at (per the Y'shtola screenshot — "beacon of hope," save-the-realm dialogue, the whole arc).

**Verdict:** Competent, unbothered, allergic to pageantry. The aesthetic is "I have an axe and a chip on my shoulder, the rest is none of your business."
