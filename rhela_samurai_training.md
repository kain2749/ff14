# Rhela Vahl — Samurai Notes for Sirclucks XP

Last updated: 2026-06-27

Purpose: quick Samurai notes for using SAM as a possible Heavensward-or-newer overworld trash-killing job while leveling Sirclucks. This is **not** an endgame rotation guide and should not derail MSQ.

Primary source checked: official FFXIV Samurai job guide — https://na.finalfantasyxiv.com/jobguide/samurai/

---

## Scope

Use SAM only when deliberately grinding Sirclucks XP and when RDM does not feel like the right tool.

Default recommendation remains:

- **MSQ:** WAR
- **Sirclucks XP trash farm:** RDM first
- **SAM:** usable backup if you want melee or want to learn it slowly

SAM has the gear available, but it is more melee-flowchart than RDM. Do not treat this as a new main-job project unless it starts feeling fun.

---

## Mental model

SAM is basically:

1. Do combos.
2. Combos give **Sen** stickers.
3. Spend Sen with **Iaijutsu**.
4. Spend Kenki on extra attacks.
5. Do not overthink raid optimization while killing random overworld trash for the bird.

The three Sen are:

- **Getsu** — from the Jinpu/Gekko path
- **Ka** — from the Shifu/Kasha path
- **Setsu** — from the Yukikaze path

For simple play:

- **3 Sen = Midare Setsugekka** for single-target deletion.
- **2 Sen = Tenka Goken** for AoE packs.
- **1 Sen = Higanbana**, but mostly ignore this on trash unless the target will live a while.

## Single-target trash loop

Use this when killing one real mob at a time.

### Basic flow

```text
Hakaze/Gyofu → Jinpu → Gekko
Hakaze/Gyofu → Shifu → Kasha
Hakaze/Gyofu → Yukikaze
Iaijutsu → Midare Setsugekka
```

Notes:

- **Jinpu → Gekko** gives your damage buff path and Getsu.
- **Shifu → Kasha** gives your speed buff path and Ka.
- **Yukikaze** gives Setsu and is the short combo.
- Once you have all 3 Sen, hit **Iaijutsu** for **Midare Setsugekka**.
- If the mob dies before Midare, fine. The bird still got paid.

### Lazy priority

If you lose track:

1. Get/refresh Jinpu path.
2. Get/refresh Shifu path.
3. Use Yukikaze path.
4. Iaijutsu when 3 Sen are lit.
5. Spend excess Kenki on Shinten if available.

That is good enough for bird farming.

## AoE trash loop

Use this only when fighting multiple mobs on purpose. For Sirclucks XP, single-pulling is usually less annoying.

```text
Fuga/Fuko → Mangetsu
Fuga/Fuko → Oka
Iaijutsu → Tenka Goken
```

Notes:

- **Mangetsu** gives the damage-buff AoE path and Getsu.
- **Oka** gives the speed-buff AoE path and Ka.
- With 2 Sen, **Iaijutsu** becomes **Tenka Goken**.
- Spend Kenki on **Hissatsu: Kyuten** if available and fighting multiple mobs.

## Meikyo Shisui: the “skip the boring part” button

**Meikyo Shisui** lets you use combo finishers without doing the full combo chain first.

Practical use:

```text
Meikyo Shisui
Gekko
Kasha
Yukikaze
Iaijutsu → Midare Setsugekka
```

That is the easiest SAM burst mental model.

Do not hoard it forever. Use it when you want a mob dead fast or when restarting your flow feels annoying.

---

## Kenki spending

Kenki is the other gauge. Do not cap it if you can avoid it, but do not stare at it like a psycho either.

Simple rules:

- **Shinten** = single-target Kenki dump.
- **Kyuten** = AoE Kenki dump.
- **Gyoten** = gap closer, costs Kenki.
- **Yaten** = backstep, mostly for dodging or setting up Enhanced Enpi.
- **Third Eye** before taking a hit if you remember it; good habit, not mandatory for trash.

For casual trash farming, Shinten/Kyuten are enough.

---

## Survival buttons

SAM is not WAR. Do not pretend it is.

Use:

- **Second Wind** for direct self-heal.
- **Bloodbath** before/while attacking if taking damage.
- **Leg Sweep** to stun annoying mobs.
- **Arm's Length** if a mob is physically slapping you and you want the slow effect.
- **Third Eye** before predictable hits.

Sirclucks being healer-built helps, but melee DPS still gets punished harder than WAR if you get lazy.

## What to ignore for now

For this purpose, ignore:

- raid openers
- burst-window alignment
- positional perfection
- Higanbana on trash
- high-level SAM nuance you have not unlocked yet
- pretending SAM is mandatory because the gear exists

This is not Savage. This is chicken XP.

### AoE core


### Utility / dumps

```text
Meikyo Shisui | Shinten | Kyuten | Gyoten | Third Eye | Second Wind | Bloodbath
```

Bind these so your hand does not have to travel all over the damn keyboard.

## Actual recommendation

Use RDM first for Sirclucks because ranged trash deletion is cleaner.

Use SAM when:

- you want melee practice,
- RDM feels boring,
- the gear is already there and you want to make use of it,
- or you specifically want to learn SAM in low-stakes overworld fights.

Stop the moment it starts feeling like homework. Go back to WAR for MSQ or RDM for simpler bird XP.
